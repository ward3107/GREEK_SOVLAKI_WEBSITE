## ğŸš« What NOT to Do

âŒ **DO NOT:**
- Pre-check consent boxes (violates Israeli spam law)
- Make marketing consent mandatory for basic service
- Hide marketing consent in small print
- Use vague language like "send me updates" (be specific about channels)
- Bundle multiple consents into one checkbox

âœ… **DO:**
- Keep checkboxes unchecked by default
- Make language clear and specific
- Allow users to consent to marketing separately from Terms
- Provide easy withdrawal options
- Keep records of when/how consent was obtained
