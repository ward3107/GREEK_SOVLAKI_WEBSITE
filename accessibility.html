Accessibility Statement - Short English Summary

(This is a non-binding summary. The Hebrew version above is the legally binding document.)

1. Commitment

Greek Souvlaki is committed to making its website accessible to people with disabilities, in accordance with Israeli regulations (Equal Rights for Persons with Disabilities) and international standards (WCAG 2.1 Level AA, Israeli Standard 5568).

2. Accessibility Features

  • Keyboard navigation: Full keyboard support with visible focus indicators
  • Screen readers: Semantic headings, ARIA landmarks, alt text for images
  • Contrast: 4.5:1 ratio for text, information not conveyed by color alone
  • Text scaling: Up to 200% without loss of functionality
  • Language support: Hebrew, Arabic, Russian, English with RTL/LTR switching

3. Supported Platforms

  • Browsers: Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari (latest versions)
  • Screen readers: NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver, TalkBack
  • Mobile: iOS (Safari, Chrome), Android (Chrome, Firefox)

4. Known Limitations

  • Some external images (Unsplash) may lack detailed alt text
  • Third-party integrations (maps, embedded videos) have partial accessibility
  • Legacy PDFs (if any) may not be fully accessible
